WONDERCORNER


Explore the questions we don't know and wonder about the world.

“Learning, especially today … is about putting one’s own words to the world, or finding one’s own voice, and exchanging our ideas with others.” -- Seymour Papert

As adults, we are less likely so ask questions and seek out help.
Somewhere along the way of growing up, we lose the openness of our former curiosity… We think we are supposed to have all the answers and we become embarrassed about what we don’t know.

Wonder Corner is an invitation to leave that shame behind.
It is a particpatory installation where you can either ASK THE QUESTIONS YOU ARE EMBARRASSED TO ASK or ANSWER THOSE OF OTHERS. It is a private and safe space for you to wonder about the world.

As Albert Einstein said, “it is important not to lose a holy curiosity: never stop questioning.”

Thesis project by Ruta Kruliauskaite at Interactive Telecommunications Program, NYU.
